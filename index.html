F-Camp

F-Camp is a new student welcoming event held annually by previous students with the companionship and mentorship of IC-PDP. F-Camp is always the most anticipated event for FPT students, not only bringing excitement and novelty, but F-Camp also spread the inherent enthusiasm and dynamism spirit of FPT students.

F-Camp 2021 with the theme "4 Musketeers" will bring a new wind to the welcoming festival, blowing into the hearts of K16 students the most positive energy, the freshest, marking the milestone. 10 years of transmitting fire in the hearts of new students in particular and all FPTU students in general. Since then, generations of students can connect and share together, creating favorable steps for the learning and living journey of new students at FPT University.

Join us, to make F-Camp 2021 a part of your youth!
